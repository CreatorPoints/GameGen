Meet the Team

The minds behind Photon Studios

Studio Overview

Photon Studios is an independent game development team founded on the principles of innovation, quality, and passion for storytelling. We are a small but dedicated group, united by our love for creating unique and engaging interactive experiences.

Our mission is to craft games that not only entertain but also challenge players and leave a lasting impression. We believe in the power of games as an art form and strive to contribute meaningfully to the medium.

Current Focus

Our main project is Portal: The Last Dance, a fan-made continuation of the acclaimed Portal series.

  • Goal: Deliver a high-quality, story-driven puzzle experience.
  • Features: New characters, innovative mechanics, and a compelling narrative set within the Portal universe.
  • Status: Actively in development.

We are also constantly brainstorming and prototyping ideas for future projects, exploring new genres and technologies.

Core Values

  • Creativity: Pushing boundaries and exploring unique ideas.
  • Quality: Meticulous attention to detail and polish.
  • Player-Centricity: Designing experiences with the player in mind.
  • Passion: Genuine enthusiasm for game development.
  • Integrity: Honesty and transparency in our work.
