About Photon Studios

Who We Are

Photon Studios is a passionate independent game development team dedicated to pushing the boundaries of interactive storytelling and innovative gameplay. Founded by a group of enthusiasts who share a love for science fiction, puzzles, and engaging narratives, we strive to create unique and memorable gaming experiences.

Our studio operates with a core belief in quality over quantity, focusing on deeply polished projects that resonate with players long after they've finished playing.

What We're Working On

Currently, our primary focus is on the development of Portal: The Last Dance.

  • Genre: Puzzle
  • Setting: A continuation of the beloved Portal universe.
  • Goal: To deliver a fan-made experience that honors the original while introducing new challenges, characters, and story elements.

We are also exploring concepts for future projects, always looking for opportunities to experiment with new mechanics and genres.
